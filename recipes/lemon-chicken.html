Lemon Chicken

Main Ingredients

  • 14 oz chicken breast
  • 3 whole lemon
  • 1 dash sesame seed
  • 12 oz corn oil

Chicken Marinade

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 0.25 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp water

Batter

  • 11 tsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 0.50 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 6 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp corn oil

Lemon Sauce

  • 2 oz lemon juice (from fresh lemons)
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 5 tbsp water
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 1.5 tsp cornstarch
  • 1.5 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp corn oil
